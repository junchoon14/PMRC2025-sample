Public Management Research Conference 2025

From June 27-30 the PMRC 2023 will take place in Travel, hosted by the Travel School of Governance (USG). On this website you can find all the information you need to plan your trip and participate in the conference. A good place to start are the frequently asked questions (FAQs). You can also find information about the conference location (here) and possibilities regarding accommodation (here). There is a provisional program for the conference itself (here) and a selection of pre-conference workshops (here) for which you can sign up upon registration. Information about registration can be found here.

The Public Management Research Conference (PMRC) is the foremost gathering of public management experts in the world. The PMRC is sponsored by the Public Management Research Association (PMRA), which publishes the Journal of Public Administration Research and Theory (JPART), one of the premier journals in the field, as well as Perspectives on Public Management and Governance (PPMG), which debuted in 2017. We look forward to welcoming you in Travel!
